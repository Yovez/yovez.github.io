About Me.

I'm an experienced Java programmer and more importantly a seasoned Bukkit/Spigot developer. I have over 4 years of Bukkit/Spigot development experience, and have made well over 100 different plugins. My only public plugin I've released has over 20,000 total downloads, which is an addon for ASkyBlock/BSkyBlock. I've completed over 50 freelance plugins and have maintained nearly a 5 Star average rating.

My Goals.

Get the idea you have, and shape it into a fully functional plugin down to the tee of exactly what you want. I try to make little-to-no compromises to give you a top notch plugin for an exceptional price, so that way in the end we both walk away happy! I will get all the details and information from you, and shape the information into a fully functional and bug-free Minecraft server plugin (using the Bukkit & Spigot APIs). The plugin may support versions 1.6.x - 1.16.x, depending on what you require out of the plugin. Additional libraries and API support can be added to the plugin.
